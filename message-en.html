Message

Message from the CEO

Thank you for visiting the website of PAGI Co., Ltd.

Our company operates through two core business pillars — Marketing and Financial Consulting — serving as a trusted partner in driving our clients' business growth.

In our Marketing practice, we specialize in the e-commerce and online retail space, providing end-to-end support from strategic planning to execution and direction, tailored to the unique characteristics of each product category. In our Financial Consulting practice, we offer M&A advisory, investment advisory, and management consulting services, supporting both growth strategy and capital strategy.

Beyond Japan, we deliver our services across growth markets including Southeast Asia and Africa, leveraging our deep understanding of local business environments to support our clients' global expansion.

"Pagi" means "morning" in Indonesian and Malay. Just as each new morning brings fresh possibilities, we aspire to be the starting point for new value creation and sustainable growth for our clients.

We look forward to working with you.

PAGI Co., Ltd. CEO
